# AI Decisions 2026-03-29

## Decision 1

Use one shared AI platform for all policy domains instead of one isolated solution per domain.

## Decision 2

Phase 1 skill training uses retrieval plus governed prompts, not model fine-tuning.

## Decision 3

Permissions must be split into:

- AI capability permission
- knowledge base permission
- skill permission

## Decision 4

AI tokens are configured through UI but must be stored in cipher or encrypted form in database. UI should display masked token only.

## Decision 5

Every policy-answering and analysis feature should support citations.

## Decision 6

Talent policy and lecture team policy should be implemented as separate knowledge bases on top of one shared AI access zone.
